About TCB

The story behind the chair

TCB was founded in 2026 by Hannah Stucky on the principle that everyone, regardless of age, gender, race, or ability, should be able to receive a five-star barbershop experience.

With 10+ years of experience in the hair industry, Hannah loves to create custom haircuts that work for you. She specializes in classic barbering, beard shaping, and family services. She is enthusiastic about continuing education and new trends in the barber industry.

In 2018, she traveled to England to study traditional straight razor shaving, earning a certificate from The London School of Barbering. In 2020, she received her certification in Barbicide's Covid-19 Infection Control Course.

Whenever she is not behind the chair, she spends her time gardening, traveling, practicing yoga, going out for live music, or playing video games.
